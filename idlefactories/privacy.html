Idle Factories Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 23 August 2025

Idle Factories (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Idle Factories does not collect any personal data from users. All gameplay and user activity remain entirely on your device.

2. Gameplay Data

Idle Factories is an offline idle/management game. All factory progress, upgrades, and game data are stored locally on your device.

  • No gameplay progress is uploaded to our servers.
  • Game saves remain only on your device unless you choose to back them up using your platform’s built-in services (such as Google Play or iCloud).
  • We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your gameplay data.

3. Analytics and Advertising

Idle Factories does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking tools. Your gameplay is private and local to your device.

4. Data Security

Because Idle Factories does not collect personal data, there is no stored data to secure on our servers. All progress and gameplay information is handled locally on your device.
