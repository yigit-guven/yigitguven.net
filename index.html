Mundus Online

30K+ Visits
2K+ Active Players
1.5K+ Discord Members
Mundus Online is a massive 3D social sandbox game on Roblox where players can build, plant, and trade in a player-driven economy. Plant seeds, splice them to discover new blocks, and grow your empire! Claim unique world names before anyone else and protect your creations with various lock systems.

Key Features

Advanced plant breeding and genetics system
100% player-driven economy
Advanced security and lock systems
Massive multiplayer social experience

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mundus Online free to play?
Yes, Mundus Online is completely free to play on Roblox. Some optional premium features are available through Roblox's in-game purchase system.
Can I play on mobile?
Yes! Mundus Online is fully compatible with PC, mobile, and tablet devices through the Roblox platform.

Petting Mod

200K+ Downloads
4.7/5 Rating
1K+ Servers
Petting is a popular Minecraft mod that adds a unique companion system. Pet mobs and have them become protective companions that follow you, offering support and assistance during your adventures. With advanced AI behavior and customization options, it's one of the most loved pet mods in the Minecraft community.

Key Features

Advanced AI behavior for companions
Highly customizable through config files
Emotional bonding system with pets
Combat support and protection

Frequently Asked Questions

Which Minecraft versions are supported?
Petting supports Minecraft 1.16.5 through 1.20.1, with regular updates for new versions.
Is it compatible with other mods?
Yes, Petting is designed to be compatible with most popular Minecraft mods and modpacks.

Hytale Economy

50+ Servers Using
100% Uptime
24/7 Support
A lightweight and persistent economy system for Hytale that adds player wallets, balance checking, and secure money transfers. Designed specifically for the Hytale modding ecosystem, this plugin provides server owners with a robust economic foundation for their custom game modes and servers.

Key Features

Persistent data storage with SQLite/MySQL
Secure transactions and anti-cheat measures
API for custom integration
Lightweight and optimized performance

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hytale Economy open source?
Yes! The entire source code is available on GitHub under the MIT license.
Does it support multiple currencies?
Yes, the system supports multiple currency types and custom naming through configuration files.
