Sudosei Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 23 August 2025

Sudosei (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Sudosei does not collect any personal data from users. All gameplay and user activity remain entirely on your device.

2. Sudoku Sharing Feature

Sudosei allows you to create your own Sudoku puzzles and share them using a generated puzzle code.

  • The puzzle code contains only the Sudoku puzzle data you created.
  • Sharing a puzzle is entirely optional. We do not collect, store, or transmit any puzzle codes to our servers.
  • When you share a code with another user, it is up to you and the recipient to manage it. The app itself does not track or save shared codes.

3. Analytics and Advertising

Sudosei does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking tools. Your gameplay is private and local to your device.

4. Data Security

Because Sudosei does not collect personal data, there is no stored data to secure on our servers. All puzzle creation, solving, and optional sharing is handled locally on your device.
