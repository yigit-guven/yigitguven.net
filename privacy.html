This Privacy Policy explains the privacy practices for yigitguven.net, a static website hosted on GitHub Pages.

No Data Collection

This website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages, which means:

  • No tracking scripts are used (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)

  • No personal information is collected
  • No form data is submitted or stored

This data is collected by GitHub, not by me, and is subject to GitHub's Privacy Policy.

This website may contain links to external sites that are not operated by me. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. I strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

I have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Theme Preference

The only "data" stored by this website is your theme preference (light or dark mode) in your browser's local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
