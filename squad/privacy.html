Squad Bot - Privacy Policy

Squad Bot values your privacy. Here’s how we handle data:

1. Data Collection

Squad does not collect or store any personal data outside Discord servers. All actions occur within your server and your Discord environment.

2. Message Data

Messages sent in channels or via bot commands are processed temporarily to perform the requested actions. None of this data is saved on external servers.

3. Member Information

Squad only accesses member information necessary to perform its functions, such as usernames or roles, but does not store or share it externally.

4. Third-Party Services

Squad does not use external analytics or tracking tools. No third-party service receives your data from the bot.

5. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of Squad indicates acceptance of any updates.
